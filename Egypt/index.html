Great Pyramids of Giza

The origin of when the pyramids of Giza were built can be traced back to many centuries, when the king Khufu was the king of ancient Egypt. The first pyramids were built around 2550 BC in honor of Khufu's father (Sneferu), who died in 2551 BC. To lure more about the pyramids you can go to the culture sector.
